Door To Door Services

Dasmesh’ Doot-to-Door services offers a wide range of urgent on-demand door-to-door courier solutions for clients of all sizes and needs. We provide priority delivery services within cities, between cities and between countries. We serve our growing roster of clients by being a reliable single source for fast, competitively priced same-day delivery.


Our comprehensive and secure door-to-door collection and delivery service ensures the safe delivery of your goods anywhere in the world. At Dasmesh, we understand that not all shipments require the same service. That's why we offer Door-to-Airport and Door-to-Door service nationwide and globally, giving our customers the option to have their cargo picked up from their residence or commercial business facility. Our Door-to-Airport and Door-to-Door service is a value added solution that provides a simple and hassle-free shipping experience.



Dasmesh Door-to-Door services can take full liability for your shipments whilst in transit, or limited liability, should you already be covered for transportation. While your unique house airway bill number allows you to track your shipment online.
Secure delivery from collection to airport or airline vault for destinations outside our network is also available upon request.
Evidences of insurance are available upon request. These can be issued per shipment, or on an annual basis depending upon the needs of your company, or even your clients. Evidences of insurance are fully authorized by our underwriters and are issued by our own in-house insurance broker.
Our cost effective solution will save time and money while enjoying the reliable personal service you’ve come to expect from Dasmesh.
